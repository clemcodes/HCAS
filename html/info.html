Disabled Access

Three steep steps from the front street to the front door make the cottage impossible for someone in a wheelchair to access easily. But if you can manage the steps we do have: Ground floor double-bed-settee (Please contact us when booking if you wish to use this), downstairs bathroom with shower, toilet and wash basin. There are also a few steps in the garden up to the play-barn and patio.
