This is a lovely place to spend a week. The back garden is enclosed and a bit of a sun trap, you can play billiards in the barn, cook in the kitchen, Read or snooze in the front room – watch TV, listen to music, play games…. There are plenty of places to eat out if you want – Fish and Chips, Chinese, Indian, Italian, Tea rooms and Cafes…. There is a Coop and a Lidl supermarket within walking distance as well as local shops, a flea market, plenty of pubs, a museum, woods, the Castle, the North Yorkshire Moors Railway, Flamingo Land is not far… Scarborough and the Coast is under 20 miles away - lots more attractions too numerous to mention there.
