About HCAS

The Hull Children's Adventure Society bought the cottage in 1983. Since then we have provided more than 1,000 holidays to families in need of a break. We get annual grants from the Sir James Reckitt charity and Headway the brain injury association which enable us to meet most of the running costs of the cottage. Families reach us through social workers and teachers in Hull and through Headway groups. We offer the holidays to people who cannot usually afford to pay for a holiday because of their financial circumstances. We reserve school holiday weeks for families with school age children. The cottage is looked after by the charity's trustees and volunteers. We employ a cleaner to clean the cottage between visits and to wash the bedding.

How to donate: We welcome donations which we use to improve furniture, equipment, games and books. Please send your donation to: Kevin Curley, 6 Cumberhills Road, Duffield Belper DE56 4HA. The cheque or postal order should be made payable to Hull Children's Adventure society.
